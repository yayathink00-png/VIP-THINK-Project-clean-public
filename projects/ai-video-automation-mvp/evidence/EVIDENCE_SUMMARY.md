# Evidence Summary

## What Can Be Reported

- A complete guarded workflow was designed for AI video production.
- The first production video flow progressed through Segment 01-05, including rerun/take handling and final edit acceptance.
- A later four-segment production validation completed the first three story segments and isolated a CTA narration stability issue.
- Automation covers state checks, name binding, safe continuation, edit support, package delivery, and review notification direction.
- Human confirmation gates were built into the operating model.
- Known risks were documented with practical next actions rather than hidden.
- The project created a reusable handoff shape for future AI video batches.
- A stable teammate handoff package was produced, with a README, pipeline overview, start prompt, and governance boundaries.
- Public-safe reporting now captures both workflow capability and quality blockers, not just successful output claims.

## What Is Intentionally Not Shown Here

- generated video outputs
- source-media references
- private prompt packs
- review records containing sensitive creative context
- real workspace URLs and dialogue/page identifiers
- browser session state
- notification credentials

## Why Evidence Is Summarized

AI video assets, prompt packs, account state, and platform identifiers have copyright, brand, and operational security boundaries. The clean repo documents the work without expanding asset risk.
