# Asset Governance Rules

## Asset Defaults

All non-source assets are private until approved.

This includes:
- images
- video
- audio
- PDFs
- slides
- spreadsheets
- CSV exports
- HTML exports
- ZIP or packaged outputs
- screenshots
- generated AI materials

## Approval Requirements

An asset may be public only when a maintainer confirms:
- ownership or license is clear
- no personal data is visible
- no customer, student, parent, supplier, or account data is visible
- no internal platform URLs, IDs, tokens, tables, dashboards, screenshots, or workflow traces are visible
- brand and commercial-use rights are approved
- AI-generated content does not imitate protected source material without permission

## Naming

Use explicit public-safe names:
- `demo-public-*`
- `sample-synthetic-*`
- `template-*`

Avoid names implying internal, raw, final, export, checkpoint, client, customer, token, account, or production material.
